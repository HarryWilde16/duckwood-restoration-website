About Us

At Duckwood Restoration, we specialize in the art and science of log home restoration. With over 23 years of hands-on experience, we’ve brought new life to hundreds of log and timber homes — from cozy cabins to fine architectural estates.

Our team provides full-service restoration, including:

  • Professional bleaching and cleaning to remove weathering and mold
  • Precision sanding and staining to protect and highlight natural wood beauty
  • Expert log repair and replacement for structural integrity and longevity

Whether your log home needs a light refinish or a complete restoration, we take pride in preserving its natural warmth and timeless craftsmanship.

Based in Whistler, BC, we proudly serve homeowners across the Sea to Sky Corridor, combining proven restoration techniques with a deep respect for every home’s unique story.
